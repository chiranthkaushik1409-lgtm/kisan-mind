## 🚨 Problem Statement

Many farmers, especially in rural areas, face difficulties identifying crop diseases and making informed farming decisions.

Challenges include:
- Lack of accessible advisory tools
- Language barriers
- Limited awareness of crop diseases
- Overuse of fertilizers and pesticides
- Dependence on guesswork for crop care

These issues can lead to crop loss, water waste, and reduced productivity.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**KisanMind** is a multilingual farming assistant prototype where users can:
- Select their crop
- Describe crop-related problems
- Receive basic advisory recommendations
- View suggestions in their preferred language

The project focuses on simplicity, accessibility, and farmer-friendly interaction.
